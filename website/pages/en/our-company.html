About.IoTeX

loTeX is the world's first privacy-centric blockchain platform that is fast, lexible, and Internet of Things (IoT) friendly. Designed and optimized for IoT, IoTeX uses state-of-the-art privacy, consensus, and subchain innovations to capture the full potential of IoT. By enabling trusted data, interoperability, and M2M automation, IoTeX connects the physical and dijital worlds and brings trusted machine economies to the masses
